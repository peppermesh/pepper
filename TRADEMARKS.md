"PepperMesh" and the PepperMesh logos are identifiers of the PepperMesh project.

You may use the PepperMesh name truthfully to:

- refer to the unmodified PepperMesh project;
- state that software is compatible with PepperMesh;
- state that software is based on or derived from PepperMesh;
- link to the official PepperMesh project.

You may not use the PepperMesh name or logo in a way that is likely to imply that a modified distribution, hosted service, product, or organization is the official PepperMesh project without permission.

Examples of generally acceptable descriptive use include:

- "compatible with PepperMesh";
- "powered by PepperMesh";
- "a fork of PepperMesh";
- "supports the PepperMesh protocol."

Examples requiring permission include:

- naming an independent hosted service "PepperMesh Cloud";
- presenting a modified distribution as the official PepperMesh distribution;
- using the official logo as the primary identity of an unrelated commercial product.
